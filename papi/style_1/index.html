What is PAPI

Papi is an innovative AI-powered finance tracker designed to make money management effortless and intuitive. Whether you're a student, a young professional, or simply someone trying to stay on top of your spending, Papi is your go-to financial assistant. What makes Papi stand out is its ability to record expenses through various input methods — you can type them in, speak them out loud, take a picture of your receipt, or even upload an audio recording. Behind the scenes, powerful AI processes this information, categorizes your expenses, and updates your budget in real time. More than just tracking, Papi gives you smart suggestions on what you can and can't afford, helping you avoid overspending and build better financial habits. With its sleek interface, multi-input flexibility, and intelligent budgeting insights, Papi turns the complex world of finance into something simple, personal, and empowering.
